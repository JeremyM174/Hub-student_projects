* Produire de premières recommandations

* Démarrer de zéro en allant chercher des données réelles

* Rester en adéquation avec les règles afférentes aux scripts (bots) des différentes sources de données

* Parvenir à exploiter et harmoniser l'ensemble de ces données pour produire des recommandations concrètes, avec un top 5 destinations et un top 20 hôtels dans la zone

Welcome to my repository dedicated to the Kayak project, for Jedha's certification!

### :airplane: The theme

Kayak is a travel search engine that helps user plan their next trip at the best price.

The marketing team discovered following user research that a majority of their customers planning a trip would like to have more information about the destinations they are recommended about.

Therefore, the company would like to produce an application capable of sourcing real weather and hotel data, starting with a list of the 35 most recommended destinations in France according to travel professionals.

### :dart: The objectives

As the project barely begins without any dataset, several tasks already await:

* Collect the data of several destinations, the hotels in the area and the weather forecast

* Store, extract, transform and load ready-to-use data from a data warehouse

* Produce the first recommendations with a top 5 destinations and a top 20 hotels in the area

### :boxing_glove: The challenges

* Start from zero and gather real data

* Comply with the various robots rules of your data sources

* Access and harmonize the gathered data to produce realistic recommendations
